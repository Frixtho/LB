Our Story

Lounusa Beach Resort was built with a simple vision: to create a peaceful retreat where guests can reconnect with nature while enjoying modern comfort.

Located above crystal-clear waters, our resort offers endless ocean views, gentle sea breezes, and a peaceful atmosphere—perfect for relaxation and unforgettable moments.
